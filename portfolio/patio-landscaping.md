## Project Overview

This comprehensive outdoor living space project transformed a backyard into a beautiful patio and landscaped area perfect for entertaining and relaxation.

## Project Details

- **Duration**: 6 weeks
- **Budget**: $28,000
- **Location**: Laval, QC
- **Year Completed**: 2024

## Scope of Work

- Natural stone patio installation (800 sq ft)
- Custom pergola with integrated lighting
- Landscaping with native plants and trees
- Outdoor kitchen prep with electrical rough-in
- Irrigation system installation
- Retaining wall construction
- Privacy fence installation
- Stone steps and pathways
- Drainage solutions and grading

## Design Features

- Natural stone patio with multiple seating areas
- Integrated lighting for evening ambiance
- Native plant selection for low maintenance
- Outdoor furniture integration
- Custom pergola with retractable awning
- Built-in planters and seating walls

## Environmental Considerations

- Permeable patio materials to reduce runoff
- Native plant selection to support local wildlife
- Efficient irrigation with weather-based controls
- Sustainable materials throughout

## Results

The client now has a stunning outdoor living space that extends their home's living area and provides a perfect venue for family gatherings and entertaining friends.
