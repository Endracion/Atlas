## Project Overview

This modern office renovation transformed a traditional corporate space into an open, collaborative work environment that reflects the company's innovative culture and values.

## Project Details

- **Duration**: 12 weeks
- **Budget**: $75,000
- **Location**: Montreal, QC
- **Year Completed**: 2024

## Scope of Work

- Complete interior demolition and reconstruction
- Open office layout with modular workstations
- Glass conference rooms and private offices
- Modern reception area with custom millwork
- Integrated technology infrastructure
- High-efficiency LED lighting systems
- Raised floor installation for cable management
- Acoustic ceiling and wall treatments
- Custom branding and signage integration

## Design Features

- Flexible meeting spaces with moveable partitions
- Ergonomic workstation design
- Collaboration zones with casual seating
- Modern materials including glass, steel, and wood
- Biophilic design elements with living walls
- Smart building controls and automation

## Results

The renovation increased employee satisfaction and productivity while providing a professional image that attracts clients and talent. The flexible design allows for future growth and reconfiguration.
